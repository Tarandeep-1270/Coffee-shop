Our Concept

The speciality about the coffee shop is not only our coffee but the idea behind this. It is the first environment friendly coffee shop in the region where noplastic would be used at all. And cups made of decomposable material are only used. The interior of the café also gives you a feel that you are sitting in nature’s lap.

Our Values

One of main values is to save the planet and in accordance to that, for every coffee that will be sold on this shop, 2 cents would be donated towards the Keep Green Organization. Another one is moving towards a sustainable and ecofriendly environment by adopting natural products by eliminating the use of plastic completely.
